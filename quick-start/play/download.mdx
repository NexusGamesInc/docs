<Warning>
  Nexus TCG is currently only available as a mod to Tabletop Simulator.
</Warning>

## How to get Nexus TCG

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Tabletop Simulator">
    1. Buy Tabletop Simulator if you don't already own it. [Steam
    Store](https://store.steampowered.com/app/286160/Tabletop_Simulator/).
    2. Download Tabletop Simulator.
    <Tip>While Tabletop Simulator is $20, it's often on sale.</Tip>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Subscribe to Nexus TCG">
    1. Go to the **Nexus Trading Card Game** mod page on [Steam
    Workshop](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3279618215).
    2. Subscribe to the mod.
    <Frame caption="Click the Subscribe button.">
      <img src="/images/download/tabletop-simulator.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a Multiplayer Server">
    1. Open Tabletop Simulator.
    2. From the main menu, create a multiplayer server.
    3. Select **Nexus Trading Card Game** from the **Workshop** menu.

    <Frame caption="Create a multiplayer server.">
      <img src="/images/download/tabletop-simulator.png" />
    </Frame>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Play Nexus TCG">
    1. Invite a friend from your Steam Friends list.
    2. Play Nexus TCG together!
  </Step>
</Steps>
