In Nullshapers, the battles that play out during each match takes place on a planet.
Each planet has biomes that affect how players play the game.

<Info>
  The final release of Nullshapers TCG will have players visiting a new planet, with
  new characters, stories, and mechanics multiple times a year.
</Info>
